VoIP Solutions FAQs

What is VoIP and how does it work?
VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) converts voice into digital signals transmitted over the internet, eliminating traditional phone lines for cost savings and flexibility.
Do I need special equipment for VoIP?
No hardware required! Use softphones on computers/mobile or compatible IP phones. We support all SIP devices.
What internet speed do I need?
100 Kbps per call recommended. Our adaptive codecs ensure quality even on slower connections.
Can I use VoIP for international calls?
Yes! Enjoy ultra-low rates to 150+ countries with premium routing for crystal-clear quality.
Is VoIP secure for business use?
Absolutely. Features TLS/SRTP encryption, fraud monitoring, and compliance with GDPR/HIPAA.
