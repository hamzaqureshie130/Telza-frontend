SIP Trunking FAQs

What is SIP Trunking?
SIP Trunking replaces physical phone lines with virtual connections over the internet, linking your PBX to the PSTN via SIP protocol.
How do I set it up?
Provide your PBX IP, and we configure the trunk. Most setups complete in under an hour with our guides.
What PBX systems are supported?
All SIP-compliant: Asterisk, FreePBX, 3CX, Cisco, Avaya, and more.
Is emergency calling supported?
Yes, with E911 registration and dynamic location updates.
How is billing handled?
Pay-per-channel or metered usage. No contracts, volume discounts available.
